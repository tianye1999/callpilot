Common questions

Why does the app ask for a pairing code?

CallPilot does not use a shared account password. Generate a short-lived code from your own Edge computer, enter it once on the phone, and keep the Edge online. The code expires and cannot be reused.

Messages or call records will not refresh

Confirm the Edge is running, the modem is online, and the phone has network access. Pull to refresh. If authorization was revoked, pair again with a new code. Content access may be disabled separately from calling.

A call says the line is offline or busy

Reconnect the USB modem, wait for mobile network registration, and end any active AI or mobile call before trying again. CallPilot cannot place emergency calls.

The other party or I cannot hear audio

Check microphone permission, raise call volume, and compare receiver and speaker output. Keep the app in the foreground for the current beta incoming-call flow.

The carrier restricted my SIM

Follow the carrier's identity-verification instructions. Avoid repeated rapid test calls. CallPilot does not bypass carrier limits or anti-fraud controls.

How do I remove local content?

Use Settings to clear mobile content or unpair. Delete call records from the Edge. If a phone is lost, revoke it from the Edge; an offline phone clears protected content after it reconnects and receives the revocation.

Security reports

Email support@bondings.ai with the subject beginning SECURITY. Do not disclose an unpatched vulnerability publicly. We aim to acknowledge complete reports within 48 hours.

常见问题

为什么需要配对码?

CallPilot 不使用共享账号密码。请从自己的 Edge 电脑生成短期配对码,在手机输入一次并保持 Edge 在线。配对码会过期且不能重复使用。

短信或通话记录无法刷新

确认 Edge 正在运行、模组在线且手机网络正常,然后下拉刷新。授权已撤销时需要重新配对;内容读取也可能与通话能力分开关闭。

拨号提示线路离线或占用

重新连接 USB 模组,等待移动网络注册,并结束正在进行的 AI 或手机通话。CallPilot 不支持紧急呼叫。

通话没有声音或音量小

检查麦克风权限并提高通话音量,对比听筒与扬声器。当前 Beta 的来电接管需要 App 保持前台。

SIM 被运营商限制

请按运营商指引完成身份核验,避免短时间反复测试拨号。CallPilot 不绕过运营商限制或反诈风控。

如何删除本地内容?

在设置中清除手机内容或解除配对,并在 Edge 删除通话记录。手机遗失时从 Edge 撤销;离线手机再次联网并收到授权失效后才会清除受保护缓存。

安全问题
