## English (U.S.)

Subtitle:

> Your SIM, remotely connected

Promotional text:

> Use your own SIM from iPhone through CallPilot Edge. Place calls, take over important inbound calls, and review SMS and AI call summaries wherever you are.

Keywords:

> remote SIM,phone,AI calls,SMS,call log,modem,voice,assistant

Description:

> ATI CallPilot is the iPhone companion for CallPilot Edge. Keep a supported modem and your own SIM connected to a computer, then securely use that line from your iPhone.
>
> REMOTE CALLING
> - Place calls through your own SIM while away from the modem.
> - Use a familiar in-call keypad, mute, speaker, and hang-up controls.
> - Take over an important inbound call after your Edge AI answers and triages it.
>
> MESSAGES AND CALL HISTORY
> - Read SMS delivered to your Edge.
> - Review call history, AI transcripts, triage results, and summaries.
> - Keep a protected local cache for recent content and clear it from Settings at any time.
>
> PRIVACY-FIRST ARCHITECTURE
> - Message bodies, transcripts, summaries, and optional recordings remain on your Edge computer.
> - The cloud control plane relays requested content without storing it in the content database or application logs.
> - Device access is revocable and content reading has separate authorization.
>
> REQUIREMENTS
> ATI CallPilot is not a standalone carrier service. It requires CallPilot Edge running on a compatible computer, a supported cellular modem, your own active SIM and carrier plan, internet access, and a one-time pairing code generated by that Edge. Carrier charges may apply. CallPilot does not support emergency calling.
